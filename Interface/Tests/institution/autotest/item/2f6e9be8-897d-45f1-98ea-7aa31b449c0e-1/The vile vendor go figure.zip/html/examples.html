Your likelihood examples

Now you try to think of some examples:

What are some things that are likely to happen tomorrow?
Remember, if it is likely, then it will probably happen, but you don't know for sure.

What are some things that are unlikely to happen tomorrow?
Remember, if it is unlikely, then it probably will not happen, but you don't know for sure.

What are some things that are certain to happen tomorrow?
Remember, if it is certain to happen, then you know for sure that it will happen.

What are some things that will certainly not happen tomorrow?
Remember, if it will certainly not happen, then you know for sure that it will not happen.

What are some things that have an equal chance of happening tomorrow?
Remember, if it has an equal chance of happening, then it is just as likely to happen as it is not to happen.
